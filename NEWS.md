## Season 4 Launch

### 26 August 2025

Welcome to Luna Framework v4.0.

This update introduces a complete redesign of the player interface, bringing a cleaner layout, smoother navigation and a more modern visual style across the entire framework. The new dashboard has been built to provide quick access to the information players care about most while creating a stronger foundation for future systems.

Alongside the new dashboard, we've introduced account integration, progression tracking, daily rewards and streak support. These systems are designed to work together, allowing players to build long-term progression while giving servers more flexibility when creating custom experiences.

Behind the scenes, significant improvements have been made to the framework architecture, networking and API layers. These changes improve performance, simplify future development and provide a more reliable platform for upcoming updates.

This release marks the beginning of a new chapter for Luna Framework. While v4.0 delivers the foundation, future updates will expand on progression, achievements, rewards, account functionality and additional quality-of-life improvements throughout the ecosystem.

Thank you to everyone testing, providing feedback and helping shape the next generation of Luna Framework.
